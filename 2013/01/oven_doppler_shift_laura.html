Calculating the mode velocity (vm=2mkbT−−−−−−√ 1) of a beam of calcium atoms m=40 amu, at temperature T=870 K (∼600∘C):

1: Using the Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution

In [32]:
 
amu = 1.6605E-27 #Atomic mass unit (kg)
k_b = 1.3807E-23 #Boltzmann constant (J/K)
 
m = 40*amu #Calcium mass
T = 870 #Temperature (K)
 
v_m = (2*k_b*T/m)**0.5
print 'Mode velocity is {0:.0f} m/s.'.format(v_m)
